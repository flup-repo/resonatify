## App Name

**Resonatify**

### Name Meaning
"Resonatify" evokes:
- **Sound/Audio**: Resonance is fundamental to audio and acoustics
- **Impact**: Something that resonates has lasting impact and meaning
- **Rhythm**: Resonatify helps create and maintain rhythm - perfect for scheduling and routines
- **Memory**: Things that resonate stay with us - memorable and meaningful

### Why Resonatify?
- Professional yet approachable
- Clearly conveys the audio aspect
- Memorable and distinctive
- Available domains (.app, .audio)
- Works well across cultures (simple English word)
- Scalable for future features (recording, AI, etc.)

## Visual Identity

### Icon Concept
**Recommended Direction**: Waveform + Clock Fusion
- Combines audio waveform with circular clock element
- Can show different states (active/idle) through fill/outline
- Works at small sizes (menu bar) and large (app icon)
- Monochrome template mode for macOS menu bar

**Alternative Concepts**:
1. **Sound Wave Ring**: Circular waveform suggesting both sound and time
2. **Resonant Bell**: Simple bell icon with subtle wave emanation
3. **Frequency Clock**: Clock face made of frequency bars

### Color Palette

**Primary Color**: Blue
- **Hex**: #3B82F6 (Blue 500)
- **Meaning**: Trust, reliability, calm, technology
- **Usage**: Primary buttons, active states, links

**Secondary Color**: Purple/Violet
- **Hex**: #8B5CF6 (Violet 500)
- **Meaning**: Creativity, meditation, premium
- **Usage**: Accents, highlights, special features

**Neutral Colors**:
- Light Mode: White (#FFFFFF) background, Gray (#111827-#9CA3AF) text
- Dark Mode: Dark (#111827) background, Light gray (#F9FAFB-#D1D5DB) text

**Semantic Colors**:
- Success: Green (#10B981)
- Warning: Amber (#F59E0B)
- Error: Red (#EF4444)

## Typography

**Primary Font**: System Native
- macOS: San Francisco
- Windows: Segoe UI
- Linux: Ubuntu/Roboto

**Rationale**:
- Native performance (no font loading)
- Optimal readability on each platform
- Respects user's system preferences
- Professional appearance

**Type Scale**:
- Headings: Bold, -0.01em to -0.02em tracking
- Body: Regular, normal tracking
- UI: Medium, normal tracking
- Small: Regular, 0.01em tracking

"Schedule audio files to play at specific times. Simple, reliable, beautiful."

### Full Description
"Resonatify is the simplest way to schedule audio files on your Mac. Create audio reminders for meditation, work breaks, practice sessions, or any routine that needs a sound cue.

**Features:**
- Schedule any audio file (MP3, WAV, FLAC, M4A, OGG)
- Flexible repeat patterns (daily, weekdays, custom)
- Runs silently in menu bar
- Native macOS notifications
- Beautiful, minimal interface
- Launch at login
- Free and open source

Perfect for:
- Meditation and mindfulness practitioners
- Remote workers needing break reminders
- Yoga instructors timing class segments
- Musicians scheduling practice sessions
- Anyone building audio-based habits

Built with Tauri, React, and Rust for maximum performance and reliability."
